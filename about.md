# About Me
Hello! I'm Wenyi, a Computer Engineering student interested in software 
engineering, mainly low-level programming and embedded systems. I think it's really
fun to work close to hardware and learn how things operate under the hood. It brings
about chances to solve interesting problems involving performance, concurrency and
resource constraints.

I'm currently working as an Embedded Software Intern at Ambiq Micro where I work on
their chips' USB functions from the Hardware Abstraction Layer to the USB Stack and
the device testing.

Outside of school and work, I take pics once in while and when my wallet isn't looking
so sad I try and travel to experience new things and learn about different cultures.
Badly maintained photography instragram account [here](https://www.instagram.com/wenyis.right.index).
